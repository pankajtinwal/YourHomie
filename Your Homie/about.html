Our Story

Empowering local professionals to build better communities.

Our vision is to build an ecosystem where local home service professionals can thrive, establish meaningful client relationships, and deliver world-class service without platform friction.

We believe that finding a reliable plumber, maid, or electrician should be as easy as sending a text to a friend. Your Homie is designed to be exactly that—your friendly neighbor who always knows an expert.
